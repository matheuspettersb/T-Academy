Windfall Island

Windfall Island is a recurring location in The Legend of Zelda series. It is a market town in the Great Sea, and is known for having the highest population of all the islands in the game. People from all over the world come to Windfall Island, and as such, the town is always lively. Many sailors have found their way here, although their ships are never around.
